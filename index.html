What are Sports ?

Sports are any activity Involving physical exertion in which two or more teams compete with each other.

Created by : Avneesh Jagtap and FYCM 1 Batch a Roll number 1 to 10.

Football

A sport in which 2 teams of 11 players score goals using a ball.

Basketball

Game played between two teams of five players each on a rectangular court.

Hockey

Each team plays with ten field players and a goalkeeper.

Kabaddi

Played between two teams of seven players, the objective of the game is for a single player on offence

Volleyball

A team sport in which two teams of six players are separated by a net.

Throwball

Throw ball is a non-contact ball sport played across a net between two teams of nine players on a rectangular court.

Baseball

Baseball is a bat-and-ball game played between two opposing teams, of nine players each, that take turns batting and fielding.
